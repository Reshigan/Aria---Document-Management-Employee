## 🎯 PART 5: RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATION

### 5.1 Technical Risks

#### **Risk 1: Bot Development Takes Longer Than Expected** ⚠️

**Probability**: 60% (common in AI projects)

**Impact**: **HIGH** (delays go-to-market, burns cash, misses revenue targets)

**Mitigation**:
- Build **bot development framework** first (reusable components)
- Start with **simplest bots** (proof of execution)
- Hire **experienced AI/ML engineers** (not juniors)
- Plan for **25% time buffer** (3 weeks → 4 weeks per bot)

#### **Risk 2: Bot Accuracy Below Market Expectations** ⚠️

**Probability**: 40% (ARIA has track record of 90%+ accuracy)

**Impact**: **MEDIUM** (churn, bad reviews, slow adoption)

**Mitigation**:
- Set **minimum accuracy targets** (85% for beta, 90% for production)
- Implement **human-in-the-loop** for low-confidence predictions
- Build **continuous learning** from user corrections
- Offer **accuracy guarantees** (money-back if <85% accuracy)

#### **Risk 3: Integration Complexity with Third-Party Systems** ⚠️

**Probability**: 70% (API changes, authentication issues, data format variations)

**Impact**: **HIGH** (customer onboarding delays, support burden)

**Mitigation**:
- Focus on **standard APIs first** (Xero, QuickBooks, Sage)
- Build **robust error handling** and logging
- Create **integration health monitoring** dashboard
- Partner with **integration specialists** (Zapier, Make, Integromat)
