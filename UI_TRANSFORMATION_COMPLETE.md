## 🎬 Animations Implemented

### Entrance Animations
- **fadeIn**: Opacity transition (0.5s)
- **slideUp**: Vertical movement (0.5s)
- **slideDown**: Top to position (0.5s)
- **slideInLeft**: Left to center (0.5s)
- **slideInRight**: Right to center (0.5s)
- **scaleIn**: Zoom effect (0.4s)

### Continuous Animations
- **float**: Vertical oscillation (3s infinite)
- **pulse**: Opacity breathing (2s infinite)
- **glow**: Shadow expansion (2s infinite)
- **bounce**: Jump effect (1.4s infinite)
- **shimmer**: Loading effect (1.5s infinite)

### Interaction Animations
- **Hover**: Transform + shadow
- **Click**: Scale down feedback
- **Focus**: Border highlight
- **Loading**: Spinner + dots
