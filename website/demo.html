Ask ARIA - Natural Language Interface

Step 1: Ask in Natural Language

Simply type what you want to do. ARIA understands natural language and converts it into ERP actions.

Example Commands:
  • "Create a quote for 100 widgets at $50 each"
  • "Show me overdue invoices"
  • "Add a new customer: Tech Solutions Inc"
  • "What's my cash position?"
  • "Run payroll for December"

Step 2: ARIA Validates & Confirms

ARIA parses your request, validates against business rules, and shows you what will be created.

ARIA Response:
I'll create an invoice with these details:

Customer: Acme Corp
Amount: $5,000.00
Description: Consulting services
Due Date: January 26, 2026 (Net 30)

GL Entries:
  DR: Accounts Receivable  $5,000.00
  CR: Consulting Revenue   $5,000.00

Shall I proceed?
What ARIA Did:

Looked up customer, applied default payment terms, determined correct GL accounts, and prepared the journal entry.

Step 3: Invoice Created & Posted

The invoice is created, GL entries are posted, and you receive confirmation with the document.

Confirmation:
Invoice INV-2024-0156 created successfully!

Customer: Acme Corp
Amount: $5,000.00
Status: Sent
Due Date: January 26, 2026

Actions completed:
  ✓ Invoice created
  ✓ GL entries posted
  ✓ Email sent to customer
  ✓ PDF generated
