Detailed Use Case Flows

📝 Create a Customer Invoice

Generate and send a professional invoice to your customer.

  1. Navigate to Sales → Invoices or ask ARIA "Create invoice"
  2. Select customer from dropdown or type name
  3. Add line items with products/services and quantities
  4. Review totals, taxes, and payment terms
  5. Click "Create & Send" to email the invoice
  6. Invoice is posted to GL automatically

💳 Record a Payment

Record customer payment and apply to outstanding invoices.

  1. Go to Sales → Payments or ask ARIA "Record payment"
  2. Select customer and payment method
  3. Enter payment amount and reference
  4. Select invoices to apply payment to
  5. Confirm and save
  6. Bank and AR accounts updated automatically

📦 Create a Purchase Order

Order goods from suppliers with proper approval workflow.

  1. Navigate to Purchasing → Purchase Orders
  2. Select supplier from list
  3. Add products with quantities and prices
  4. Set delivery date and shipping address
  5. Submit for approval (if required)
  6. Send PO to supplier via email

📥 Receive Goods

Record goods receipt against a purchase order.

  1. Go to Purchasing → Goods Receipts
  2. Select the purchase order
  3. Enter quantities received
  4. Note any discrepancies or damages
  5. Confirm receipt
  6. Inventory updated automatically

🏦 Bank Reconciliation

Match bank transactions with your accounting records.

  1. Go to Banking → Reconciliation
  2. Import bank statement (CSV/OFX)
  3. Review auto-matched transactions
  4. Manually match remaining items
  5. Create new transactions for unmatched items
  6. Complete reconciliation

👥 Add a New Employee

Onboard a new employee with all required information.

  1. Navigate to HR → Employees
  2. Click "Add Employee"
  3. Enter personal details and contact info
  4. Set department, position, and manager
  5. Configure payroll details and tax info
  6. Set up system access and permissions

💵 Run Payroll

Process monthly payroll with automatic tax calculations.

  1. Go to HR → Payroll → Run Payroll
  2. Select pay period and employees
  3. Review hours, leave, and adjustments
  4. System calculates taxes and deductions
  5. Approve payroll run
  6. Generate bank file and payslips

📈 Generate Financial Reports

Create trial balance, income statement, and balance sheet.

  1. Navigate to Reports → Financial
  2. Select report type
  3. Choose date range and filters
  4. Click "Generate Report"
  5. View on screen or export to Excel/PDF
  6. Schedule for automatic generation

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within minutes.

Can I import data from my old system?

Yes! Aria supports CSV and Excel imports for customers, suppliers, products, and opening balances. Use the Import Wizard in Settings.

How do I add more users?

Go to Settings → Users → Add User. Note that user limits depend on your subscription plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice with Net 30 terms.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Upgrade. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption, SOC2 compliance, and your data is stored in secure Cloudflare data centers.
