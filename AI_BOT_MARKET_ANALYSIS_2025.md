   - Don't wait for perfection
   - Get real customer feedback
   - Build what customers actually need

### ❌ **DON'T DO THESE THINGS**

1. **Don't Try to Compete on Price**
   - Market leaders have economies of scale
   - You can't win a price war
   - Focus on value, not cost

2. **Don't Build Everything**
   - You don't need 200 integrations
   - Start with 5-10 most important
   - Use Zapier/Make for the rest

   - 60% of users expect mobile access
   - At minimum, mobile-responsive web

4. **Don't Skimp on Security**
   - Enterprise deals die on security reviews
   - Get SOC 2 certified ASAP
   - Hire a security consultant

5. **Don't Go It Alone**
   - You need at least 3-4 people minimum
   - Trying to solo this will take years
   - Outsource what you can
