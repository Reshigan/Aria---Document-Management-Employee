ARIA is not competing head-to-head with UiPath/SAP on bot count or enterprise features. Instead, ARIA created a **new market category:**

```
Traditional RPA:   "500+ bots, global, enterprise"
```

**Why this works:**
- SA market needs BBBEE compliance (70% of enterprises)
- SMEs can't afford UiPath (R50-100K/year)
- No competitor has BBBEE bot (unique globally)
- Fast deployment wins (24 hours vs months)
