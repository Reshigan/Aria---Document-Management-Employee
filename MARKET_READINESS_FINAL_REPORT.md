## ⚠️ RISKS & MITIGATION

### Identified Risks

#### 1. Market Education Risk
**Risk**: Customers don't understand AI bots or automation value  
**Likelihood**: Medium  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Create educational content (blog, videos)
- Offer free ROI calculator
- Provide free trials with onboarding
- Case studies showing before/after

#### 2. Integration Complexity Risk
**Risk**: Customers have legacy systems that don't integrate easily  
**Likelihood**: High  
**Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**:
- Build top 10 connectors first (Xero, Sage, etc.)
- Offer CSV import/export as fallback
- Manual data migration service (paid)
- Partner with implementation consultants

#### 3. Competitor Response Risk
**Risk**: UiPath/Microsoft notice and compete on price  
**Likelihood**: Low (we're too small to notice)  
**Impact**: High (if it happens)  
**Mitigation**:
- Focus on SA-specific features (moat)
- Build customer relationships (high switching cost)
- Move fast to capture market share
- Patent BBBEE compliance bot features

#### 4. Pricing Risk
**Risk**: Price too high (no sales) or too low (can't sustain)  
**Likelihood**: Medium  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Beta testing with multiple pricing tiers
- Flexible pricing based on customer feedback
- Annual contracts with lock-in discounts
- Value-based pricing (% of savings)

#### 5. Regulatory Risk
**Risk**: POPIA, SARS, or other regulations change  
**Likelihood**: Medium  
**Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**:
- Legal review of all compliance features
- Regular updates to match regulation changes
- Clear data processing agreements
- Compliance certification (if needed)
