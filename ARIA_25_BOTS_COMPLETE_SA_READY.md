## 🇿🇦 SOUTH AFRICAN COMPLIANCE

### Why This Matters

**South Africa has unique requirements that Aria now handles:**

1. **BBBEE (B-BBEE)** - Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment
   - Government contracts require minimum BBBEE level
   - Large companies prefer Level 1-4 suppliers
   - Complex calculations (5 elements, 109 points total)
   - **Annual verification required**
   - **Laws change yearly** → Aria auto-updates!

2. **Payroll (SARS Compliance)**
   - Different tax system than US/EU
   - **PAYE** (Pay As You Earn) - Progressive tax (18-45%)
   - **UIF** - Unemployment Insurance (1%)
   - **SDL** - Skills Development Levy (1%)
   - **ETI** - Employment Tax Incentive
   - **Tax year**: March 1 - February 28 (not Jan-Dec!)
   - **Annual tax table updates** → Aria auto-adjusts!

3. **Reporting**
   - IRP5 (annual tax certificate for employees)
   - EMP201 (monthly SARS return)
   - BBBEE certificate (annual verification)

### How It Works

```
Email arrives → aria@vantax.com
  ↓
Microsoft Graph API webhook
  ↓
Aria Email Service
  ↓
Identify sender (customer/supplier/employee)
  ↓
Parse intent using AI
  ↓
Route to appropriate bot (via Meta-Bot)
  ↓
Bot processes request
  ↓
Send response email
```

**Customer needs help:**
- Email: customer@acme.com → support@vantax.com
- Aria: Identifies customer, looks up order status
- Response: "Your order shipped yesterday, tracking: UPS-12345"

**Employee requests PTO:**
- Email: jane@vantax.com → hr@vantax.com
