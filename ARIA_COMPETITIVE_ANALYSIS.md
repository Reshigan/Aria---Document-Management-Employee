**The Problem:**
- Every SA company needs BBBEE compliance
- Government contracts require minimum level
- Large companies prefer Level 1-4 suppliers
- Annual verification required (R50K-R150K/year)
- Complex calculations (5 elements, 109 points)
- Laws change annually → manual recalculation

**Competitors:**
- SAP: ❌ No BBBEE automation
- Odoo: ❌ No BBBEE automation
- Dynamics: ❌ No BBBEE automation
- NetSuite: ❌ No BBBEE automation

**Aria:**
- ✅ Automatic BBBEE scorecard calculation
- ✅ Supplier BBBEE verification
- ✅ Preferential procurement tracking
- ✅ Skills development reporting
- ✅ Annual law updates (auto-adjust!)
- ✅ Certificate generation
- ✅ **Save R100K+/year on verification**

**→ This ALONE justifies Aria in South Africa!** 🏆

---

**2. SARS Payroll Compliance** ⭐⭐

**The Problem:**
- South African tax system is unique
- PAYE (progressive tax: 18-45%)
- UIF ceiling (updates annually)
- SDL (1%)
- ETI (complex calculations)
- Tax year: March-February (not Jan-Dec!)
- IRP5, EMP201 submissions
- Manual errors = SARS penalties

**Competitors:**
- SAP: ⚠️ Has SA payroll (expensive module)
- Odoo: ⚠️ 3rd party module (limited)
- Dynamics: ⚠️ Limited SA support
- NetSuite: ❌ No SA payroll
- Xero: ⚠️ Basic payroll (limited)

**Aria:**
- ✅ Full SARS compliance
- ✅ PAYE auto-calculation
- ✅ UIF auto-calculation
- ✅ SDL auto-calculation
- ✅ ETI auto-calculation
- ✅ IRP5 generation
- ✅ EMP201 filing
- ✅ Annual tax table updates
- ✅ **100% accuracy, zero penalties**
